Design Statement

Typography:

The first TypeFace picked for the project was Alfa Slab One, a slab serif, for h1 and h2 portions. This TypeFace is futuristic and grabs attention. Big bold letters. I wanted a font that matched with the theme of the website, Sci-Fi related.

The second TypeFace, for the paragraph portions is: Special Gothic. This font is sleek, modern, and easy to read.
