Mitski

Mitski is a singer and songwriter. She self released her first two albums when she was in college. I would like to discuss with her her inspirations for the albums and lyrics in her songs. She’s had an interesting career as someone who got famous quite fast.

Brian Cox

Brian Cox is an astrophysicist. He often researches black holes, and other space related topics. I saw a lecture of his a few years back, and I would like to see him again. He breaks down complicated topics very simply and in a fun way. It would be food for thought party too.

AOC

I also couldn’t pass up the opportunity to see Alexandra Ocasio-Cortez, known as AOC, if given the chance. She used to be a waitress, as I used to be a waitress, which is a common ground. She is very inspirational and I like her values.
