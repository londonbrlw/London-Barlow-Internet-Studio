Introducing: The Queens

An all womens dodgeball team. These characters were chosen under the assumption that cheating is not allowed

Evelyn Wang

From the movie everything everywhere all at once. While cheating is not allowed, is it cheating if she summons the skills of a pro dodgeball player? It's technically still Evelyn playing the game. The refs have allowed it. This makes her the perfect canidate for our main offense.

Avatar Korra

From avatar: the legend of Korra. Korra is strong witted and defensive. She is also very strong, good for defense.

Violet

From Arcane: the series. Violet is also a strong female character. She would be able to hold defense down well.

Gwen Stacy

From into the spiderverse. I picked gwen due to her skills as a superhero. She would likely be quick, and make good offense.

Garnet

From Stephen Universe. Garnet is the perfect leader. She would direct the team and hold everything together.
